## Design Context

### Users
Both recruiters/hiring managers evaluating for job opportunities AND fellow engineers and open-source community members who might collaborate. The profile needs to work for technical and non-technical viewers alike.

### Brand Personality
Bold, creative, sharp. The interface should stand out, show personality, and be memorable — not safe or generic.

### Aesthetic Direction
Editorial and typographic — personality through writing and structure, not decorative widgets. Amber brand color (`hsl(36, 90%, 50%)` / `#E89419`) carried from the portfolio site at navarrolajous.com. Dark background tones from `hsl(20, 14%, 10%)`. Clean, brutalist leanings with intentional restraint.

Anti-references: typing animations, badge walls, snake contribution graphs, trophy widgets, gradient text, glassmorphism, cyan-on-dark AI aesthetic, visitor counters.

### Design Principles
1. **Every element earns its spot** — no decorative filler, no sections that exist just because "everyone has one"
2. **Personality through voice** — first-person, concrete, opinionated writing over ornamental visuals
3. **Lean over loud** — three projects, one stats card, two lines for talks. Confidence in editing.
4. **Brand continuity** — amber accent and warm dark tones match the portfolio site
5. **Asymmetry and restraint** — left-aligned text, varied spacing, no centering-everything default
